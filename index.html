How it works

STEP 01 Try up to $1,000

Not sure what size, color, or style is good for you? Browse and choose up to $1,000 in qualifying items labeled "Try Before You Buy" to add to your cart.

STEP 02 Checkout

You will not be charged full-price. We'll simply authorize your card with a $15 refundable deposit. Oh, did we mention the Free Shipping? Yup, you're all set!

STEP 03 Try for 10 days

Love the convenience of online shopping but miss being able to try in a fitting room? Your living room now is your fitting room. Worry free and risk free - We are here to support you until we find your perfect fit.

STEP 04 Pay for what you keep

Didn't fit? No worries! Make an online Return Request within 10 days after receiving the items. You only pay for what you keep. The $15 deposit will directly apply on the chosen items. Send back what doesn't work for Free. Simple!
